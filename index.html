NGOF-MAA has now been working since 2000 but every year when we look forward it seems like there is lot more to be done. It is well said that,"Our silent presence can be more powerful than a million empty words.


NGOF-MAA has been able to bring smiles over million faces and wishes to maintain this harmony way too long, motto behind our work is to spread happiness along with giving life's first experiences to the underprivileged ones.


We all know that having our life's first experience about something is truly heartwarming. Thus, we try to give the first experience just like the first trip of Metro, the first visit to Research Centre, the first experience of having pizza and so on.

Diwali

Diwali Celebrations

Every Diwali we have been conducting a event for underprivileged people who have no one celebrate with them. It has now been the crucial annual event for us. We distribute clothes, Diwali snacks and toys to the children all over Nagpur Slums. The event has its utmost attraction because of the decorated truck running all over the Nagpur. Diwali being the biggest festivals of all time we try to celebrate it with those who need us.
